Description

Feeling like a down right nasty italiano tonight? Well look no further! This scrumpbibblyunctions pizza recipe will scratch that itch just the way you like it. Dig in ya little piggy!

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 1/4 cup warm water
  • 1 tsp active dry yeast
  • 1 cup cold water
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 1 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

Toppings:

  • 6 oz low moisture mozzarella cheese, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 tsp dried oregano
  • 1/4 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • 1/2 cup no salt added canned crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tbsp extra-virgin olive oil
  • 6 fresh leaves basil, torn

Steps:

  1. Mix dough ingredients in a large bowl to make the dough
  2. Knead the dough on a floured surface until smooth, about 10 minutes
  3. Divide in half and form into two tight dough balls. Coat with olive oil and refrigerate in a sealed container for at least 16 hours.
  4. Preheat the oven, eat the dough!
