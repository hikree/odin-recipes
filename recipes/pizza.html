Pizza Recipe

Description

It's Saturday night and you're feeling juuuuuuuuust a little too tired to go out. Look no further, for this simple and quick margherita pizza will make you feel like a chef without breaking the bank! If you're looking for other ways to spice up this recipe, try your own toppings! One of my favorite combinations is Mama Lil's peppers and a drizzle of honey.

Ingredients

  • Pizza Dough
  • 1/2 heaping cup pizza sauce
  • 8 oz fresh bocconcini mozzarella, sliced
  • 1/2 cup sliced cherry tomatoes
  • 10 basil leaves
  • Pinch red pepper flakes
  • Extra virgin olive oil for drizzling

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 500°F
  2. Spread the pizza sauce onto the dough
  3. Top with the fresh mozzarella and tomatoes
  4. Bake 10 to 12 minutes, or until crust is browned
  5. Remove from oven and top with fresh basil leaves and a pinch of red pepper flakes. Drizzle with olive oil and serve.
