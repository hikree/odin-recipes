Berry Smoothie Recipe

Home

Description

When I'm in the mood for something fast and refreshing (and surprisingly healthy) I go straight for a berry smoothie. This recipe may be simple but boy does it hit the spot!

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups apple juice (can also use almond milk, skim milk, coconut milk, or other flavor of juice)
  • 1 banana, sliced
  • 1 1/2 cup frozen mixed berries
  • 3/4 cup greek yogurt
  • 1 tbsp honey (optional)

Steps

  1. Add apple juice, banana, berries, and yogurt to blender; blend until smooth. If the smoothie is too thick, add more liquid until desired consistency is reached.
  2. Taste and add honey if desired. Pour into two glassesh and garnish with fresh berries and mint sprigs if desired.
